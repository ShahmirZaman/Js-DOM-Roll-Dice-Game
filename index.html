Roll Dice Game

Game Guidelines

  • Players have to score by clicking roll dice button.
  • If dice number will be 1 then the player turn will be switch on the otherhand current score of player will be 0.
  • For this,player should used the hold button so that its score will be not 0 and the turn will also switch.
  • Last but not least, from both of players whose score reached to 50 first then that player will win the game.
